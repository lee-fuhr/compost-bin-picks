Composting at home cuts kitchen waste and creates nutrient-rich soil for your garden, but you don’t need to spend a fortune to start. Budget-friendly compost bins make this eco-friendly habit accessible to everyone. When choosing a bin on a budget, focus on three things: material durability (thick plastic or galvanized steel last longer), capacity (a 7- to 10-cubic-foot bin suits most households), and ease of turning, tumbling bins save your back, while stationary bins cost less. Avoid cheap models with thin walls that crack in winter or gaps that let pests in.
